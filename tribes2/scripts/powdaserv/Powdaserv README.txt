HOW TO INSTALL:

Unzip the powdaserv.mrc file into your MAIN mirc directory and then enter "/load -rs powdaserv.mrc" in the chatbox (where you always type in IRC) and hit enter. To use simply right click on a channel background or a name in the nicklist.
